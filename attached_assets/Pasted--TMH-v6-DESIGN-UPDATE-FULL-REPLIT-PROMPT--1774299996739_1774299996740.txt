3. NEW SECTION: PREDICTIONS
─────────────────────────────────────────

Add a new section between the Debates section and the Questions
strip. This is a distinct product mechanic from polls.

Section eyebrow: "PREDICTIONS"
Section title: "What Do You Think Actually Happens?"
Section sub: "Not what should happen. What will."

A Prediction is different from a Poll:
  - A Poll asks for an OPINION (no right answer)
  - A Prediction asks you to forecast a REAL FUTURE EVENT
    (there will be a definitive answer when it happens)

Prediction cards look similar to debate cards but with:
  - A DEADLINE badge ("Resolves: Ramadan 2026" or "Resolves: Dec 2026")
    Style: amber/gold background rgba(251,191,36,0.15),
    border rgba(251,191,36,0.3), text #F59E0B
    Barlow Condensed 700, 0.62rem, uppercase
  - A PREDICTION RESULT badge when closed:
    Green: "Called it" or Red: "Region was wrong"
  - Instead of option buttons, TWO bold binary choices:
    YES / NO or two specific outcomes
    Each takes full width, large text
  - A confidence meter below (% of people who said Yes vs No)
    Two bars side by side, crimson vs white/grey
  - CTA: "Lock In Your Prediction →"
